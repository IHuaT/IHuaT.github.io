About

I am an Assistant Professor in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Prairie View A&M University. My research lies at the intersection of AI, cyber-physical systems, and smart healthcare, with an emphasis on efficient and trustworthy learning for physiological sensing (e.g., ECG) and real-time monitoring.

GitHub · Google Scholar · CV (PDF) · LinkedIn

Research

  • ECG & physiological signal analytics: transformer/CNN models, feature modules, beat-by-beat classification.
  • Smart healthcare CPS: real-time sensing, wireless/Bluetooth data acquisition, multi-modal integration.
  • Efficient & trustworthy ML: energy-aware inference, robustness, bias/privacy considerations for deployment.
