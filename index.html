About

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Cybersecurity at the University of Central Missouri. My research focuses on AI-driven approaches for real-time physiological signal analysis, with particular emphasis on beat-by-beat ECG classification, smart healthcare systems, and efficient deep learning deployment.

My work integrates transformer models, feature engineering, machine learning, and cyber-physical system design to enable reliable and scalable health monitoring. I am also expanding research into Agentic AI systems for healthcare portals and public-facing AI tools.

Research Areas

  • AI for ECG & Cardiac Health: Beat-by-beat classification, transformer-based models, computational efficiency.
  • Smart Healthcare Systems: Real-time AI deployment, IoT integration, physiological sensing platforms.
  • Trustworthy & Applied AI: Privacy-aware AI interfaces, CodeBERT cybersecurity applications, deepfake detection.
