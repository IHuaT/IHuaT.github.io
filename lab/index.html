Mission

The Smart Healthcare AI Lab develops AI methods and systems that translate into reliable healthcare technologies. We focus on real-time physiological signal understanding (especially ECG), efficient deep learning for deployment, and human-centered interfaces that support trustworthy interaction with medical AI tools.

Projects

Agentic AI in Smart Healthcare (Ongoing)

2026–2027

We are developing an Agentic AI system that supports smart healthcare workflows through a web portal and phone-based interface. The goal is to provide safe, privacy-aware, task-oriented assistance (e.g., structured follow-ups, symptom / context capture, and evidence-grounded responses).

  • Interfaces: website + phone portal (conversational access)
  • Technical focus: tool use, planning, retrieval, evaluation, safety guardrails
  • Impact: scalable access + better patient communication and triage support
  • How students contribute: prototype modules, datasets/evaluation, UX + privacy design
